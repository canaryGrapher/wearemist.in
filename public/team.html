How to be a part of MIST?

We are recruiting on September 16, 17, and 18. These are the steps you need to follow to get in: Forget what branch you are a part of in MIT. Look at the Poster at https://www.instagram.com/p/B2SDOxnhc_Z/?igshid=5n17rbxb4y19 and decide what domain you want to be a part of. Even if you cannot make up your mind, we'll help you understand and find your interest. Before 5:45 pm, reach the venue, i.e., AB5 - 312 and AB5 - 313. Go there and take your seat. You'll be given a question paper to solve and 60 minutes on the clock(trust us, you can finish it in 30). If you can cheat, cheat! There is one thing common among the best hackers in the world. They are brilliant cheaters. We'll let you know if you got in after everyone has taken the test and we have evaluated the papers. You can keep track of the results on our Instagram page https://instagram.com/_wearemist_?igshid=1lm0plp1saxib and our Facebook page https://www.facebook.com/wearemist.in/ You can always attend our workshops to keep yourselves updated.
