How to be a part of MIST?

  • We announce out recruitment dates every odd semester. These are the steps you need to follow to get in:

  • We don't care about your educational background. Cybersecurity is for everyone.

  • We have multiple domains. Decide what domain you want to be a part of. Even if you cannot make up your mind, we'll help you understand and find your interest.

  • Reach the venue on time. Venue and time will be on our posters.

  • You'll be given a question paper to solve and 60 minutes on the clock(trust us, you can finish it in 30).

  • We'll let you know if you got in shortly after the test. You can keep track of the results on our Instagram and Facebook page

  • Even if you didn't get in, you can always attend our workshops to keep yourselves updated.
