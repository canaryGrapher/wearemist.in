How to be a part of MIST?

We announce our recruitment dates every odd semester. This is a brief summary of the process.

  • We're open to people of all backgrounds . Cyber Security is for everyone.
  • We have multiple domains. Decide what domain you want to be a part of. Even if you cannot make up your mind, we'll help you understand and find your interest.
  • Take the test, give your interview, sit back and wait for the results. You can keep track of the results on our Instagram and Facebook page .
  • Even if you didn't get in, you can always attend our workshops to keep yourselves updated.
